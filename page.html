What is GPT-3?
GPT-3 (Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3) is a natural language processing model developed by OpenAI. It is capable of generating human-like text and can be used for a variety of tasks, including language translation, question answering, and text completion.
How does GPT-3 work?
GPT-3 uses a transformer-based neural network architecture to generate text. The model is pre-trained on a large corpus of text and then fine-tuned on specific tasks, allowing it to generate high-quality text for a wide range of applications.
